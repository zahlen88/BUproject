Our Services

We design and build a wide range of barn structures to meet every need. From traditional pole barns to modern barndominiums, Barns Unlimited has you covered. Explore our offerings below:

Pole Barns

Pole barns are multi-use post-frame buildings that are economical and versatile. Whether you need a farm storage shed, a horse barn, or a large garage, our pole barns are built to exacting standards:contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}. We customize size, layout, doors, windows, and finishing options to suit your specific application. With quality lumber framing and durable metal cladding, Barns Unlimited pole barns are built to last and can fulfill a variety of residential or commercial needs.

Steel Truss Hybrids

Steel Truss Hybrid barns combine wooden posts with engineered steel trusses. This design allows for wider spans and higher ceilings, ideal for large workshops, equipment storage, or agricultural buildings. The steel trusses provide superior strength and open interiors free of support posts. Our hybrid approach means you get the **best of both worlds** – the charm of a pole barn with the muscle of a steel building. We ensure the wood and steel components work together seamlessly for a durable, cost-effective structure.

Vintage Barns

Have an old barn that needs a new life? Our team specializes in historic barn restoration and preservation. We carefully repair or replace structural members, re-side and re-roof with period-appropriate materials, and preserve as much original character as possible. If you dream of an **authentic vintage-style barn**, we can also build new barns with classic designs – like timber-frame barns or gambrel-roofed icons – so you get the look of yesteryear with the reliability of new construction. Our commitment to historic preservation means your vintage barn project is in passionate, skilled hands.

DIY Barn Packages

For the hands-on customer, we offer custom DIY barn building kits. We will design your barn, prepare the blueprints, and provide all the materials you need as a package – delivered to your site. These **DIY packages** include premium lumber, trusses, metal roofing and siding panels, fasteners, and detailed instructions. Each package is tailored to your size and style specifications, not a one-size-fits-all kit. You get to save on labor costs and enjoy the satisfaction of building your own barn, while still using our high-quality materials and design:contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}. (Don’t worry – we’re just a phone call away if you need guidance!)

Commercial Buildings

Barns Unlimited’s expertise isn’t limited to traditional barns. We also design and construct commercial-grade post-frame and steel-frame buildings. From warehouse storage facilities to retail farm stores, our commercial projects are built to meet code requirements and your business needs. We can incorporate insulation, foundation work, custom facades, and more. The advantage of our post-frame commercial buildings is a faster construction timeline and cost savings compared to conventional construction. Strength, functionality, and professional appearance are the hallmarks of our commercial offerings.

Barndominiums

A barndominium is a modern trend that turns a barn-style structure into a beautiful home. We have experience creating barndominiums that blend rustic barn exteriors with comfortable, custom home interiors. Our team works closely with you to design an interior floor plan that meets your lifestyle – open concept living areas, kitchens, bedrooms, and all the amenities you desire – all within a durable barn shell. Barndominiums offer a unique, spacious living experience with the aesthetic charm of a barn. If you’re dreaming of country living in a barn home, Barns Unlimited can make it happen!
