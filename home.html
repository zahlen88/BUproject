Welcome to Barns Unlimited

Barns Unlimited is a family-owned and operated barn construction company in Lima, Ohio. We are committed to sustainable practices and historic preservation, with years of proven experience in barn restoration and repair. We design, construct, and restore quality barns tailored to your needs, bringing your dream barn to life with care and craftsmanship.
